I am Seeking
I am Striving
I am in it with all my heart

By: Camila S. Moreira


My Theme Explained

December 16, 2015

The quote that sums up my experience in this technical writing course, as well as what it means to me is: "I am seeking, I am striving, I am in it with my all heart" by Van Gogh. It was very difficult to come up with a theme that would summarize my efforts, as well as tie in with my ePortfolio site, but when I found this quote I started making a list of how I have been seeking, and striving in this course. It was effortless when I wrote my reflection because throughout the semester I have truly sought to learn as much as I can about technical writing guidelines, and the purpose behind this kind of writing. I have strived very much in making sure my assignments were reader-centered, and persuasive. The last statement “in it with all my heart” made sense because I found a new passion, and was able to incorporate my past passion with this course by making my ePortfolio from scratch. This quote is my identity in technical writing. I know that through all my assignments I have put a lot of effort in revising, and reviewing the proper to address an audience. My development in writing has expanded greatly by learning about technical communication, because it is a tool that everyone should have in order to create clear and meaningful articles. I am grateful for my experience in this course, and have learned many skills, and techniques that I will continue to carry throughout my writing.

- Camila Moreira

Scope
Students in the Class will test our set of instructions for adding Headers & Footers into a Microsoft Word Document.
Purpose
The purpose of this Usability Survey is to test the directions for clarity, ease and success in adding these features in a Microsoft Word Document.
Equipment
A computer, preferably a Mac, as these instructions were designed for the Appleâ€™s version of Microsoft Word. Participants Any Microsoft Word user.
Scenarios
The directions contain steps to adding Headers and Footers in Microsoft Word.
Metrics (Background question)
Participants of the Survey are asked to test the directions and give feedback on the clarity of the directions, the ease in taking the steps and finally if they were successful in adding Header and Footers to their document.
